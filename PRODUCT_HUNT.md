## Description (260 chars for PH)
Free solar vitamin D calculator. Enter your city and skin type to see today's synthesis window, how many minutes you need in the sun, and a 5-day forecast. Based on Holick/Dowdy research, real UV data, and available in 6 languages.

## First Comment (post this right after launching)

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I built Vitamina D Explorer because I was tired of generic "get 15 minutes of sun" advice. The reality is way more nuanced:

- **A person with skin type I in London** needs a completely different sun exposure than **skin type V in Madrid**
- **In winter at 52°N**, vitamin D synthesis is literally impossible — you need supplements
- **Cloud cover changes everything** — a clear day vs. overcast can mean 3x the exposure time

So I built a calculator that accounts for all of this: your exact location, skin type (Fitzpatrick I-VI), age, body area exposed, and real-time UV/weather data from Open-Meteo.

**What makes it different:**
- Science-based: uses Holick & Dowdy (2010) vitamin D estimation models
- Not just "yes/no" — tells you exactly how many minutes for your target IU
- 5-day forecast so you can plan ahead
- Global heatmap showing where synthesis is possible year-round
- Push notifications when your UV window opens
- Works in 6 languages: EN, ES, FR, DE, RU, LT
- Completely free, no account required
- PWA — works on any device

The app also recommends supplementation (D3 + K2 + magnesium) when the sun can't help, with dosing guidance from current research.

I'd love your feedback! What features would make this more useful for you?
